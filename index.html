Analysis Aim

Regional Price Index (RPI)

RPI measures price changes experienced by households in Regional Western Australia. With Perth as the index, the RPI provides a comparison between
various consumer goods and services such as food, alcohol and housing.

This is an interactive chart to show the correlation of the RPI index. The data set is based on findings from 7 years, ranging from 2000 - 2019.
Interactive plot shows different data views. To see the relationship between different factors chose the particular axis.
